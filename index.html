FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is it really a one-time purchase?
Yes. Pay once and own the app forever — that includes the Pro features built into the app. (A separate optional cloud service, ucm Connect, may be offered later.)
What happens if you stop developing the app?
The app keeps working. ucm runs entirely on your device — your data lives in your own iCloud, not our servers, so nothing depends on us staying online.
Why is it so cheap?
Because there's no server to pay for and no investors to satisfy. Other apps charge $10–15 every month forever. ucm is a one-time price — you're paying for software, not renting it.
Can I get a refund?
Yes — refunds are handled by Apple through the standard App Store process, typically within 14 days of purchase.
Is my data really private?
Completely. ucm has no account, no login, and no analytics tracking you. Your finances never touch our servers. See the privacy details above.
Will I lose my data if I switch devices?
No. ucm syncs through your private iCloud account, so your data follows you across iPhone and iPad automatically — encrypted, and only visible to you.
Is there an Android version?
Not yet — ucm is built iOS-first to take full advantage of the platform. An Android version is being considered for the future.
