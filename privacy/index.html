Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: Your financial data never leaves your devices. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. What data the app stores

ucm allows you to record personal financial transactions, set budgets, and track spending. All of this information is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data is synced between your own Apple devices through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. We have no access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage.

2. Data we do not collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Financial amounts, categories, or transaction history
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

There are no accounts, no logins, and no sign-up of any kind.

3. Receipt Scanning

ucm includes a Smart Scan receipt scanner powered entirely by Apple's on-device Vision framework. Receipt images are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external server. No internet connection is required to use this feature.

4. Third-party SDKs and frameworks

The app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. It uses only Apple's first-party frameworks (SwiftUI, SwiftData, StoreKit, CloudKit).

5. Purchases

ucm offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock Pro features. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information.

6. Data deletion

Because all data is stored on your device (and optionally in your own iCloud account), you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any server after deletion because no data was ever sent to ours.

7. Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that collect data, this policy will be updated before that feature ships and you will be notified within the app.
